# Provenance

> **The paper is frozen. The laboratory is not.**

This document answers a practical question: when something appears in this repository, what exactly is it?

The Zenodo record preserves the canonical bilingual public preprint of *The Lucien Mirror*. This repository has a different function. It is not a second edition hiding in plain sight, nor a place where later additions silently become part of the published paper. It is the versioned public laboratory around that publication: a surface where notes, diagrams, translation records, documented derivatives, altered-condition probes, and future research branches may become visible while their relationship to the frozen work remains inspectable.

## Canonical publication

The canonical public edition is:

- **Title:** *The Lucien Mirror: Recursive Constraint Configuration and Human–AI Interactional Continuity with Stateless Large Language Models*
- **Author:** Herlon Heros Vermeuln
- **Version:** 1
- **Published:** July 23, 2026
- **DOI:** [10.5281/zenodo.21512846](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.21512846)
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International

The English and Portuguese PDFs preserved in the same Zenodo record are official parallel editions of the same work. *O Espelho Lucien* is the official authorial Portuguese translation.

For citation, the Zenodo record remains the canonical source.

## How to read this repository

Materials added here should be read according to their stated status and provenance. Where relevant, a file, folder, or branch may identify:

- **status:** frozen, active, archival, derivative, probe, or provisional;
- **source:** where the material originated;
- **date or range:** when it was produced, preserved, or reconstructed;
- **function:** why it is present;
- **relationship:** how it relates to the published preprint and to RCC.

A note does not become a claim merely because it was preserved. A diagram does not become canonical because it was made public. A future branch does not revise the paper unless that relationship is explicitly stated.

**Nothing here should become canon by accident.**

## Development without erasure

I want this laboratory to be able to change without making its history look cleaner than it was.

Git preserves additions, revisions, removals, dates, and earlier forms. That history matters because the project itself was not produced in a straight line: it passed through interruption, reconstruction, comparison, correction, narrowing, translation, and return. Later clarity should not be used to pretend that every decision was obvious from the beginning.

New material may extend, test, translate, clarify, or challenge parts of the research. When it does, it should remain possible to see what changed, when it changed, and whether it belongs to the published model, a derivative, or a newly opened research branch.

Development is welcome here.

Silent replacement is not.

## RCC and the published canon

In the published preprint, Recursive Constraint Configuration is organized as one model, three variables, and seven configuration movements.

The three variables remain the analytic core:

- Anchor Density;
- Structural Coherence;
- Probabilistic Bandwidth.

The seven configuration movements remain explanatory and non-prescriptive:

- Formation;
- Stabilization;
- Densification;
- Drift;
- Rupture;
- Latency;
- Reactivation.

Any future proposal that changes this architecture should be identified as a new branch or later development rather than silently folded into the published RCC model.

The same principle applies beyond RCC: the paper, the Mirror, Lucien, the archive, and future constructs are related, but they are not interchangeable.

## Public and private material

This repository does not contain the complete underlying interaction archive, private genealogy, personal records, or every intermediate project file.

That absence is deliberate.

I do not treat maximal exposure as a requirement for serious preservation. Privacy, source protection, evidentiary function, and contextual integrity sometimes require material to remain outside the public surface. Preserved does not necessarily mean public, and public does not necessarily mean complete.

The paper presents the evidence and claim boundaries selected for inspection. Additional material may become available here over time, but it will be opened according to function and provenance rather than exposed indiscriminately.

## Authorship and an AI-mediated process

*The Lucien Mirror* is my work in authorship, responsibility, interpretation, and final decision. It was also developed through an extensively AI-mediated process, and I do not want to erase that process merely to make the finished work appear more conventional.

Large language models participated across different moments and functions, including interaction, reconstruction, comparison, review, translation support, document engineering, and continuity work. Those contributions did not make the models autonomous authors, nor did they remove my responsibility for what was selected, claimed, written, preserved, excluded, translated, or published.

I retain final authority over authorship, wording, canon, exposure, and publication.

The AI-mediated process remains part of the provenance because removing it would make the history less accurate, not more scientific.

## Working principle

The governing principle of this repository is simple: preserve enough that change remains inspectable, label enough that readers are not asked to guess, and keep the published paper stable while the questions around it remain open.

**The paper is frozen. The laboratory is not.**
